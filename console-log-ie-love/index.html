console.log & IE love

What a great JavaScript debugging tool console.log is , right? perfect for DEV!

But what happens if you release it into PROD ? ohh, the users that use IE will have a hell of an experience with your website.

Seems that console.log is not supported in IE8 & IE9 until you open developer tools :) yeah, if you press F12 and have developer tools opened it works, otherwise (if you are a normal user) it throws a JavaScript error and stops your website from working properly.

One solution you may have is to stop writing console.log or have some way to stop it from reaching production, another way may be to use: https://github.com/cpatik/console.log-wrapper

 

Do you know any other alternatives?
